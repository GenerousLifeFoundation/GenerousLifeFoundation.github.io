OUR DONATIONS SO FAR...

Our first donation was held on 26th December, 2016. This donation was accompanied by a general meeting held in Akwatia. The donation was led by Mr. Gyandoh Nkwafi Emmanuel. The second donation was held a year later on the 27th of December, 2017 at GOOD SHEPHERD OUTREACH and OSENASE ORPHANAGE which were both located in Akwatia. Both donations were led by Mr. Gyandoh Nkwafi Emmanuel. The third donation of the foundation was held on the 11th of August, 2018 at GOOD SHEPHERD OUTREACH in Akwatia. This donation was also led by Mr. Gyandoh Nkwafi Emmanuel. The fourth donation was within the same year and it took place on the 28th of December, 2018 at JEHOVAH RAPHA located in Suhum. This fourth donation was led by Mr. Clark Justice. The fifth donation of Generous Life Foundation was held on the 30th of August, 2019 at GOOD SHEPHERD OUTREACH in Akwatia. The donation was led by Mr. Adu Mensah Emmanuel. The sixth donation was held on the 27th of December, 2019 at SOS Asiakwa led by Mr. Justice Clark.
